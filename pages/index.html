City of Austin
Office of Innovation

We help you make an impact.

The City of Austin's Innovation Office works with City teams to identify and test solutions to complex challenges facing Austin.

We serve as the city government’s “incubator” for early-stage ideas and solutions. We provide facilitation, research, and design services that aim to better understand problems, create and test solutions to those problems, and make progress toward Austin’s strategic goals.

our work

Addressing complex problems in human-centered ways


We tackle thorny issues facing Austin through research and human-centered design. Our work on homelessness in Austin has provided new insights and opportunities for services and solutions that span across City programs and external collaborations.
